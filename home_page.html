Welcome to the land of Hedonia

A land broken up by two warring kingdoms, each attempting to claim supremecy over the other.

It is a land which ensures the promise of fortune, glory, and power.

Great opportunities, and even greater dangers, lie around every corner


One of those kingdoms is the Kingdom of Yiramm .

They are led by the mighty Queen Hannah, Slayer of a Thousand Men .

Built on the banks of the Titrate River, farmers make up the backbone of this kingdom.

Crops from here feed mouths in every corner of Hedonia.

Although, the army is not large, Queen Hannah can call on her vast civilian militias to bolster her forces.

What it lacks in physical power, the Kingdom of Yiramm more than makes up for in numbers.


The other is the Kingdom of Elieveth

Although, by far, the richer of the two kingdoms, hard times have befallen this kingdom.

A mysterious plague has ran unchecked through the populace and wiped out a quarter of its population.

Cmpounded with recent losses in battle. the Kingdom of Elieveth is at its lowest point ever.

The economy is in shambles, the streets are littered with thieves and brigands patrol the outskirts hoping to catch a helpless merchant.

You are the ruler of this kingdom. It is your job to restore this fallen kingdom back to its glory days.

To do so, you must destroy the Kingdom of Yiramm before it destroys you.
